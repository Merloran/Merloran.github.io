## Bug-Off!
**Team Turtle Team (6 members)**

Custom game engine built with C++ and DirectX 12, featuring ECS architecture, a level editor, AI state machines, steering behaviours, custom flowfield pathfinding, and animation systems with audio events.

Winner of Technical Greatness at Student Games Festival 2025.

**Technologies:** C++, DirectX 12, ECS, Custom Engine

## Pathtracer in Vulkan and OpenGL
**Solo project**

Monte Carlo pathtracer implementing BVH acceleration with SAH, compute shaders, BRDF materials (scattering, emissive, metallic, dielectric), importance sampling, and bindless rendering. Includes performance comparison between Vulkan and OpenGL APIs.

**Technologies:** C++, Vulkan, OpenGL, Compute Shaders, GLTF

[Vulkan Version](https://github.com/Merloran/RayTracerVulkan){: .btn} [OpenGL Version](https://github.com/Merloran/RayTracerOpenGL){: .btn}

## Silence
**Team Silence (6 members) - ZTGK Contest**

Custom 3D game engine with ECS architecture, level editor, skeletal animations, screen space reflections (SSR), volumetric lighting, BSP partitioning, 3D collisions, and decal system.

Top 3 at ZTGK 2023, special award from 11 bit studios for the editor.

**Technologies:** C++, OpenGL, ECS, Custom Engine

[Project](https://cladur.github.io/projects/silence/){: .btn} [Demo](https://cladur.itch.io/silence){: .btn}
